I loved sleeping. It has been my favorite past time for as long as I can remember. When I was a child, I would sleep in the car on our way to different places even though I had a good night's sleep. I loved sleeping in when I was young. I love falling asleep early as I got older. I would cry when I knew that sleep was going to be limited on a particularly busy night. Im sure the tears were mostly because of stress but I honestly hated sleep deprivation. I hated high school for that reason. I hated interrupted sleep. I hated having a roommate in college for that reason. I don’t like things that delay my sleep schedule. I don’t like going out late at night. 

So I got texts from my friends on our group chat, l hoped things would fall through.

>Stella: So see you guys at 10?
>Viola: Yep!
>Carla: :D
>Chelsea: Definitely!
>Me: Sounds good!

But it didn’t really sound good. It sounded exhausting and like hard work. But the girls wanted to go drink, and I loved my friends. I loved spending time with them. I loved them all so much. We grew up together, and we stuck through thick and thin. Sometimes, we put on our tightest dresses and tell ourselves we will be going to sleep at a later time for friends. 

I decided to be a designated driver for the group. The girls came by and decided they wanted to go clubbing. They brought their clothes and makeup to do here since I had full body mirrors. They arrived about half an hour before 10 so I knew that we probably weren’t getting to the club until at least an hour. 

I put on some comfortable booties and a nice matching dress. The rest of the girls got ready, some in crop tops and others in dresses. They all wore heels they would likely end up taking off at some point of the night. Either that, or this night could end early. I was a bit hopeful with the idea that this could end early. 

I drove and we parked in a well lit place in the city. We got in after being carded. I kept track of the girls, making sure they weren’t about to get messed up or taken advantage of by other people. I had a very expensive Dr. Pepper I was happily drinking and when I was done dancing for a while, I got to sit down at a nearby table. 

“Can I get you another of what you’re having?” some guy asked me over the loud music. 

I smiled politely. “No, thank you!”  

He sat down anyway. “Are you the designated driver?”

I furrowed my brow. “How did you know?”

“I promise I’m not a creep. I’ve been watching you. You have less endurance than the rest of your friends so you’re clearly not drinking. Also, your shoes seem more comfortable than theirs.”

I smiled. “Oh yeah? What am I drinking?”

He looked at my cup. “Coke?”

“Man, you almost had me going. Dr. Pepper.” I laughed. “Are you also the designated driver for your group?” I asked him.

He smiled and nodded. “Yeah. My turn this time around.”

“I’m Regina,” I told him and extended my hand.

He took it. “Michael. Nice to meet you.” 

“Ditto,” I replied, yawning.

“Ready for bed?” he asked.

I sipped some more of my Dr. Pepper before nodding. “You have no idea.”

“Oh I believe you. I’ve been awake for 24 hours.”

“You know, you’re more susceptible to being controlled when you’re sleep deprived.”

He laughed. “That’s why I was convinced to be the designated driver!” He sighed. “Yeah. Wish I could take a nap right now.”

“Me too.”

We both laughed at how meek we sounded and continued talking. When the girls decided it was time to move on, I wrote my number down on a napkin and awaited a text at an appropriate time.
