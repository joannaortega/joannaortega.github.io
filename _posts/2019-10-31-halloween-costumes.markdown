“Can we dress up as Mike and Eleven?” I asked Freddie, my boyfriend. It was going to be our first Halloween as a couple and I felt like those outfits were appropriate enough for school.

“Which season?” he asked. We’d watched the show together and we both loved it. We watched it over a weekend after we’d power-finished our homework.

“Season one is easiest I think.” I’ve seen several articles of clothing in our closet that could help.

He looked at me with a smile. “Fine, whatever you want, Helena.” 

I leapt at him, wrapping my arms around him. “Yay!”

He kissed the top of my head. “You’re so cute, Helly.” My name lended itself to too many nicknames. I’d grown to embrace the hell-related ones.

“The devil hath pleasant shape,” I murmured to him. 

He snickered. “That is very much true.”

“We are totally going to win the contest,” I said, excitedly.

“Totally. However...Mike and Eleven are overdone, especially because of how popular the show is right now.”

“Crap. That’s true.” I bit my nail. What should we be? I ran through a list of characters in my head..

“Maybe...maybe Beetlejuice and Wynona Ryder’s character?” 

“That would be cool! Or Morticia and Gomez!” 

“This went gothic so quickly,” he replied with a chuckle. 

“I could also be Helena from the MCR music video. You could totally be Gerard Way.”

He smiled. “Whichever attire we can find first or make cheapest is easiest.”
