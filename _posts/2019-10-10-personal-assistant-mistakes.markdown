I saw her in a magazine before I met her in person. She was gorgeous and on the cover of Cosmopolitan magazine. She was a model turned boss of a clothing chain. She was alluring with fierce hazel eyes and vibrant light brown hair. Her skin had a nice light tan, perfect and sun kissed. She had a nice hourglass figure, shown off by an emerald green dress that fit perfectly on her skin. She kinda stood like she was ready to take on the world. 

So when I heard that she needed a personal assistant, I leaped at the opportunity to manage her. I’d managed some male actors before so I figured it couldn’t be that hard to manage a female model. I also had a couple of friends that worked for models. They could help hook me up with any other notes or things I’m missing. I knew they would be okay with it; us personal assistants stuck together. We knew the stresses of the business. I was especially close to my friend Bryan, who’d been the PA to a particularly successful model for several years now. 

“So, I hear you’re working for Chantel now,” he said to me one afternoon we both had off. 

I smiled at him. “Yeah. She’s gorgeous, man. Talk about eye candy everytime I see her.”

“Get it together, Eli,” Bryan said, his face serious. “You can’t date your boss.”

“Why not?”

“Listen, don’t take this as me hitting on you, but you’re an attractive guy. Not even that will save you from getting fired after you make a move.”

“Alright. Alright,” I said to appease his mind. But I was bluffing.

---

Chantel was working long hours to get the new launch of another location in her empire open on time. We were spending a lot of time together. So one day, after a late burger run (yes, Chantel eats burgers), we were sitting on the floor in her office. She was shifting things around.

“You work pretty hard, Elijah,” she said to me after she sat back to eat another fry. 

I smiled at her. “You’re one to talk, boss.” 

“Yeah, well, I’m pretty sure I’ve had more PAs than you’ve had bosses.”

I chuckled. That was probably true. I moved on from my bosses because I wanted to due to boredom and not because they wanted to get rid of me.

She leaned over near my side of the floor and turned her face to me. “What do you think?”

“You’re beautiful,” I said and I kissed her. 
She froze and immediately pushed me away. “What the hell was that?”

“I...I…” Fuck.

“Get out.”

I got up and ran to my car as fast as I could. How could I have been so stupid. Fuck. Fuck. Fuck. I just started dialing numbers. 

“Hello?”

“Eli, you will not believe what I just did.”
